Teaching

I was the tutor for the 2017 Autumn semester course “Introduction to Lattice QCD” at ETH Zurich. The exercises, along with sample C++ codes to do the lattice field theory simulations and jupyter python notebooks where the resulting data are analysed and plotted, are available on github.
